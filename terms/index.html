Each fax submission consumes one credit per page at the time of sending. Credits are charged per attempt, regardless of whether the fax is successfully delivered. If the receiving fax machine is busy, unreachable, or the number is invalid, the credit is not refunded.

If a fax fails due to a temporary issue (busy line, no answer), we will automatically retry delivery up to 2 times at no additional cost.
