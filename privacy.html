I. COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

Information You Provide Directly to Us

When you create an account and use Faxr's Services, you may provide us with certain information voluntarily, which may include but is not limited to:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and any third-party platform identifiers (such as your Apple ID, Google account, or Discord account) if you choose to sign in using these services
  • Fax Transmission Data: Recipient fax numbers, fax subject lines and cover page content, the status and outcome of your faxes (queued, sent, completed, failed), page counts, timestamps, and any error messages associated with failed transmissions
  • Document Data: Documents you upload, scan, or photograph for faxing, including PDF files, images, and scanned documents captured via your device camera. Cover pages generated within the app are also collected as part of the fax transmission process
  • Subscription and Purchase Data: Your subscription plan type (weekly, monthly, or yearly), subscription status, purchase timestamps, expiration dates, transaction IDs from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, credit pack purchases, credit balance, and receipt data stored for purchase validation
  • Phone Number Management: Virtual fax numbers you rent through the Services, including the phone number, region, type, and associated costs. Fax recipient information from your device contacts, including phone numbers, country codes, and contact names
  • Feedback and Communications: When you submit feature requests, contact us for support, or provide feedback, we collect the content of your communications, including any rating or details you provide

Information We Collect Automatically

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including:

  • Device Information: Hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, app version, and crash data that helps us improve stability
  • Usage Information: Features you use within Faxr, frequency and duration of app sessions, fax sending and receiving patterns, subscription usage statistics, and interaction with push notifications
  • Technical Data: IP address (for general location and security purposes), platform identifier (iOS or Android), and other technical information necessary to provide and secure the Services

We may combine information we collect from multiple sources to provide you with a more seamless and personalized experience. When we combine this information, we treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Additionally, we may aggregate or de-identify information so that it cannot reasonably be used to identify you individually. Aggregated or de-identified information is not subject to this Privacy Policy.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes necessary to provide our Services to you:

  • Account Management and Authentication: Creating and managing your Faxr account, verifying your identity through Apple, Google, or Discord sign-in, enabling secure login, and managing your preferences and settings

  • Subscription and Credit Management: Processing subscription enrollments and renewals, managing credit pack purchases, tracking your credit balance and usage, validating purchases with Apple and Google, and providing accurate billing information
  • Communication and Notifications: Sending you notifications about fax delivery status (completed, failed), subscription alerts (expiration, renewal), credit balance alerts, and responding to your support inquiries
  • Service Improvement and Analytics: Understanding usage patterns, identifying areas for enhancement, improving app performance and stability, and developing new features

We also use information for the following additional business purposes:

  • Safety and Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents; investigating suspicious activity; enforcing our Terms of Use; and maintaining the security and integrity of our Services
  • Legal and Compliance: Responding to legal requests and court orders; complying with applicable laws and regulations including telecommunications regulations; protecting our rights, property, and interests; and maintaining records as required by law

IV. COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

VIII. DOCUMENT DATA PROCESSING

Given the sensitive nature of documents transmitted via fax, we take special care in handling your document data:

Document Handling

  • Transmission: Documents you upload or scan are securely transmitted to our fax carrier for delivery to the recipient's fax machine. Documents are encrypted in transit
  • Storage: Sent and received fax documents (PDFs) are stored in our cloud infrastructure to allow you to access your fax history. Storage retention periods depend on your subscription tier, ranging from 7 to 90 days
  • On-Device Processing: Document scanning via your device camera is processed locally on your device using Google ML Kit before being uploaded for fax transmission
  • Cover Pages: Cover pages are generated within the app using the information you provide (recipient name, subject, notes) and are included as part of the fax transmission

Document Security

  • Documents are encrypted during transmission and at rest
  • Access to stored documents requires authentication
  • Documents are automatically deleted after your subscription's retention period expires
  • We do not access, review, or analyze the content of your faxed documents for any purpose other than transmission and storage

IX. DATA RETENTION, SECURITY, AND INTEGRITY

Data Retention Periods

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the Services and comply with legal obligations. Specific retention periods include:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active, then deleted within 90 days of account deletion unless required for legal purposes
  • Fax Documents: Retained according to your subscription tier's storage retention period (7 to 90 days from the date of transmission), then automatically deleted
  • Fax Transaction Records: Metadata about your faxes (recipient numbers, timestamps, status) retained for the duration of your account plus 30 days for backup purposes
  • Subscription and Payment Records: Retained as required by applicable law, typically 7 years for financial records
  • Communication Records: Retained for 2 years for customer service purposes or as required by law

When retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize the information using industry-standard data destruction methods.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information against loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, misuse, or modification. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Data encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest for stored documents and personal information
  • Secure Storage: Authentication tokens and sensitive credentials are stored using encrypted device storage (Flutter Secure Storage)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access limitations for our personnel
  • Purchase Validation: Server-side receipt validation for in-app purchases via secure edge functions
  • Security Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats
  • Incident Response: Established procedures for security incident handling

However, please understand that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
